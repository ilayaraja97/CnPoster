# ethernet
* developed in mid 1970s by researchers at xerox palo alto research center(PARC)
* the general name for technology behind ethernet is CSMA/CD
* ethernet has its roots in an early packet radio network called aloha
* an ethernet network can't be bigger than 2500m in diameter
* each ethernet frame can contain upto 1500 bytes of data and minimum of 64 bytes
